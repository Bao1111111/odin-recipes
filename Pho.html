Pho

Description

Takes a Bit of Time to make

But very Delicious

Ingredients

  • Not a lot of Meat
  • Pho Noodles
  • Lots of green Stuff
  • Stuff for Broth
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put Water and the Stuff for the Broth in a Bucket
  2. Wait for a Day
  3. Put the Broth and everything else inside a Bole
  4. And enjoy!
